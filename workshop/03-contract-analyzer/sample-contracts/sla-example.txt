SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Between: Acme Cloud Services ("Provider") and Customer ("Client")
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
The Provider shall ensure that the Service is available 99.9% of the time,
measured on a monthly basis. Downtime caused by scheduled maintenance
shall not be included in availability calculations.

2. RESPONSE TIMES
Support requests shall be responded to in a timely manner. Critical issues
shall be addressed with reasonable urgency. The Provider agrees to use
best efforts to resolve all issues promptly.

3. DATA HANDLING
Customer data shall be stored securely and handled in accordance with
applicable regulations. Data backups shall be performed at appropriate
intervals. It shall be provided to the Customer upon request.

4. LIABILITY
The Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not be
unreasonable. In no event shall either party be liable for indirect,
incidental, or consequential damages, except where such limitation is
not unlikely to be enforceable under applicable law.

5. MODIFICATIONS
The Provider may modify the terms of this Agreement at its sole discretion
upon providing notice to the Customer. Continued use of the Service after
such modification constitutes acceptance.

6. TERM AND RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods.
They may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason.

7. SUPPORT HOURS
Technical support shall be available during business hours. Emergency
support for critical issues shall be provided outside business hours
on a best-efforts basis.

8. COMPENSATION
In the event of a service level breach, the Customer shall be entitled
to adequate compensation as determined by the Provider.
