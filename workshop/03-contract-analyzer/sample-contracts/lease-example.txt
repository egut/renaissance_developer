RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

Between: Nordic Properties AB ("Landlord") and Tenant ("Tenant")
Property: Apartment 4B, Storgatan 12, Stockholm

1. TERM
The lease shall commence on February 1, 2026 and continue for a period
of twelve (12) months. The lease shall automatically renew unless
terminated by either party with reasonable notice.

2. RENT
The Tenant agrees to pay monthly rent of SEK 12,500, due on the first
day of each month. Late payments shall incur appropriate penalties.
The Landlord may adjust the rent at any time to reflect market conditions.

3. MAINTENANCE
The Tenant shall maintain the premises in good condition. Repairs
necessitated by normal wear and tear shall be the responsibility of
the Landlord. Other repairs shall be handled in a timely fashion.
It is responsible for ensuring compliance with building codes.

4. ALTERATIONS
The Tenant must not make any alterations to the premises without
prior written consent from the Landlord, which shall not be
unreasonably withheld. The definition of "alterations" shall be
determined at the Landlord's sole discretion.

5. ACCESS
The Landlord shall have the right to access the premises for
inspections at reasonable times. Emergency access shall be permitted
at any time without prior notice.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement. The Tenant shall vacate
the premises in a timely manner upon termination. They shall ensure
the property is returned in adequate condition.

7. LIABILITY
The Landlord warrants that the premises are fit for habitation.
The Tenant is responsible for any damage beyond normal wear and tear.
Liability for shared spaces shall be determined as appropriate.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by applicable Swedish law.
Disputes shall be resolved through appropriate channels.
